# Show & Tell Template (3 minutes per person)

## 1. Who I Am (15 seconds)
- Name, role, one line on what you do

## 2. What I Built — Show the Live URL (1 minute)
- Share your screen — pull up your live Vercel URL
- Walk through: homepage → click the chat widget → show a response
- If the proposal engine works: trigger it and show the PDF / Telegram alert

## 3. The Compound — How Day 1 Fed Day 2 (45 seconds)
- Pick ONE example of Day 1 → Day 2 compounding:
  - "My voice fingerprint made the chatbot sound like me, not a template"
  - "My research shaped the hero — I positioned against competitors my researcher found"
  - "My triage rules became the lead scoring — HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW, same framework"
  - "The roast panel was my advisory board pattern from yesterday, just applied to a website"

## 4. Where I Got Stuck (30 seconds)
- What broke? What prompt or fix saved you?
- One thing you'd do differently

## 5. What I'm Doing Monday (15 seconds)
- One specific thing — not "I'll use AI more"
- Examples: "I'm running /brief every morning" or "I'm sending this URL to 3 prospects" or "I'm adding a second service page"

---

## Tips for a Great Demo
- Share your screen and show the live URL, not your code
- If the agent pipeline works end-to-end, demo it — that's the wow moment
- If something is broken, show it — failure stories are the best learning
- Be specific about prompts — "I told Claude to..." is more useful than "Claude built it"
- Keep it tight — 3 minutes goes fast
